Game Highlights:

Day 1:

Picking Up the Pieces

Following the gun play with five armed thugs, the party was in a desperate situation. With the exception of Aune, all were seriously injured. Aspen was rendered unconscious from a near-fatal chest wound, but was revived after a short while. (Note that Aspen was relatively inactive for the rest of the following day and night due to these injuries.) Calelith, and Turell were also both seriously wounded in the fight. Having expended all magic resources, the injured were bandaged as well as possible under the circumstances.

 

Following the administering of first aid, the room was thoroughly inspected by Aune. Only eight of the ten bunks in the room appeared to have been in use recently. The trunks in the room contained a variety of personal items: food, wine, and miscellaneous supplies. It was noted that the thugs did not appear to share common uniforms. An assortment of valuables were confiscated, totaling 9 Gold pieces, 27 Crowns, 13 Silver pieces, a gold necklace, a diamond earring, a ring, and a bracelet. In a gun locker were found 2 rifles, 2 pistols, and an enormous amount of ammunition (in addition to the guns used by the thugs). Searching further, a foreign crate marked "Roberto’s Find Cloths" was discovered, containing contraband clothing.

During the investigation of the room, the sole surviving thug regained consciousness. Questioned by Aune and others, he revealed that he was simply a hired hand recruited from a local town, who hauled loads and moved crates around in the underground tunnels beneath the mansion. The tunnels, he said, lead to a hidden cove, where an underground stream connects to the sea. He described some of the smuggling going on, and gave accounts of his boss, associates, and other "lizard things". When asked how many others were still in the tunnels, he admitted that there were still three others unaccounted for, and possibly some monsters. He also described two prisoners held below in the tunnels; a young girl, and an Imperial agent.

The Prisoners

The party entered the tunnels through a door in the barracks room. The injured thug was tied up and brought with them, escorted by Aspen. Aune led the way down the dark, slippery staircase, sneaking quietly. Off in the dark of the tunnel, the party heard a pair of voices discussing the fate of one of the prisoners. Calelith suddenly shown a flashlight on the two thugs, and demanded that they surrender. Instead they cursed and began running away down the tunnel. Calelith fired at the fleeing pair, wounding one in the rump. In the end, they made good their escape. With the element of surprise gone, the party quickly searched and found the two prisoners, bound but not seriously hurt. The girl turned out to be Anna, while the man appeared to be in an Imperial uniform. Aune threw them over her shoulder and the party quickly made it’s way back up the stairs into the barracks room, where they secured the door and untied the prisoners.

Anna told her story, of how she came to the Haunted Mansion to find Rick. She did find him, but she was captured and Rick was taken away. Then a second male prisoner was thrown in with her.

The male prisoner turned out to be an Imperial Border Patrol officer named Halse. He had been investigating a warehouse near Edington when he was jumped by surprise and taken here for questioning by "Sabbath". Despite being recently treated like a sack of potatoes, he gladly joined the group of Imperial Marshals in combating the thugs in the Mansion. Anna also pledged her support, and took a weapon.

 

Through the Master’s Door

Earlier, while searching the barracks room, the party had noticed a door into the Master’s room which was warded with magic runes. The party now turned its attention to getting into this room. A considerable amount of discussion and preparation ensued. In the end, the door was blown open with a shotgun and Calelith and Aune rigged up a device to toss a body (of one of the slain thugs) in through the opening. This worked, and Calelith was able to recover Halse’s magic book and sword. While searching the Master’s room, Calelith also found a box containing four potions, three of which appeared identical.

The party then began planning their next move. It was suggested that they should leave the mansion, but it was a known fact that there were Gnolls in the woods. Another suggestion was to hide in the mansion and rig booby-raps for the thugs. During these discussions, Halse persuades Calelith to try one of the three similar potions, and sure enough they turn out to be "Healing Potions". The other two potions were quickly consumed! Having healed somewhat, the party is emboldened to reenter the tunnels and clean out the thugs.

Battle with the Cellar Thugs, Round 2

Moving once more into the tunnels, the part split up to explore a fork in the passage, with Halse going one way and Calelith going the other. It was Calelith who encountered the Green Slime dripping from the ceiling, and he hurriedly removed his contaminated clothing. Taking the lead once again Calelith then tripped a grenade trap, which exploded and would have killed him if not for his "Shield" ability.

Further down the tunnels, the party heard sounds from thugs who were now alerted by the grenade trap going off. A second grenade trap was spotted, and around a bend the thugs appeared to have set up a defensive position. The second grenade trap was disarmed, and pretending to be a fellow thug Halse crept up to the bend in the tunnel. Halse then threw a grenade, and the party opened fire. Behind a barricade were three thugs and two Gnolls. The grenade was poorly placed, and did not appear to injure any of the thugs. Halse’s opening shot did take the head off of one of the thugs, but return fire badly injured Halse. Aune’s shot missed, and she too was hit with return fire. Calelith injured one thug, and fortunately return fire only grazed him. Under a hail of fire, the party retreated. Back upstairs, the doorway again was barricaded and the stairway trapped with Green Slime. (Round 2 went to the thugs.)

Bump in the Night

The party fortified their position as best they could to wait out the night. Halse set up three gun traps, one for each doorway into the Mansion. Watches were assigned, and the party tried to get what rest it could. During the second watch, Halse was alerted to the sound of a gun trap going off. Waking the party, he went to investigate. Sounds were heard upstairs of someone bumping around up there! Turell accompanied Halse to have a look around, and he spotted a skiff leaving the cliffs at the water’s edge, carrying two thugs. Also, a flashing signal light was sighted off shore, and it appeared to be a ship signaling the Mansion. (And "things" were sighted swimming in the waters!) Calelith came above to relieve Turell, who went back below. Then, Calelith’s keen night vision picked out someone making their way off into the night carrying a box.

Calelith and Halse decided to track the thug with the box. He left a trail of blood; a sign that the gun trap had done it’s job. Calelith succeeds in following the trail, which took them down to the water where they spied a single man rowing out to sea on a skiff. Calelith and Halse shot the thug off of the skiff, and the skiff slowly drifted back to shore. The box which was taken was found to contain a signal lantern, but more importantly the skiff afforded an opportunity. Calelith suggested using the skiff to sneak up behind the barricaded thugs in the tunnels. And so . . .

Battle with the Cellar Thugs, Round 3

Calelith and Halse rowed into the cave opening in the cliff face and beached the skiff at the underground cove. Not taking any chances, Calelith crept up behind the thugs and threw a grenade in a surprise attack. Halse also threw in a grenade, and the combined attack was quite successful. The large Gnolls, however, were not immediately defeated, and one began chasing Calelith. Halse managed to distract it, allowing Calelith to throw yet another grenade. Pulling his gun, Calelith then shot and killed one badly weakened Gnoll, before turning and shooting the last surviving Gnoll who was engaged in hand-to-hand combat with Halse. (Round 3 went to the Empire!)

At Game Conclusion

The party, still badly in need of rest and relaxation, had at last secured the Haunted Mansion - - for now. There is still the threat of a ship off shore, Gnolls in the woods, and things swimming in the waters. More booby-traps may still remain in the Mansion, and the party is injured and out of magic. But an new day is dawning, and who knows . . .
