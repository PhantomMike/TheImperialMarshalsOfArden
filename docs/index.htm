Introduction

This website comprises a summary of the rules and adventures of a modified D&D role playing game.   The rule modifications, fantasy world history, and each adventure of the party are all outlined here.   The focus of the site is on the actual adventures of a small party of D&D gamers in a world combining the fantasy of D&D and the technology of the period in our history circa 1919.
