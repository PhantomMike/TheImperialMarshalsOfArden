Game #3 Summary

Date: December 11, 1999
Weather: Overcast

Players in Attendance:
Mike, Diane, Kevin

Game Dates Played:
Start: May 21, 1919 (Black Bay)
Stop: May 23, 1919 (Cross roads of Main North Road and Haunted Mansion Road)
Elapsed time: 2.5 days

NPCs met:

Mrs. Brown (Anna’s Mom)
Vesla White (Anna’s best friend)
Magistrate O’Connor
Wild Woman
Friendly Parishioners (left our horses at their farm)

Game Highlights:

Day 1:

It is afternoon before we make it to the Green’s Farm. It is a large house and we are met by a field hand who takes us up to the house. We meet with Mrs. Green and ask about Rick’s disappearance. Rick had left early one morning two weeks ago when they didn’t have school. He took a horse and something out of the barn when he left. Investigations revealed he took a hacksaw, crowbar and hammer. He had had confrontations with some of the local boys, but was close to Anna. Anna had been over a lot after Rick’s disappearance. A quick search of his room uncovered some letters, some from his parents and some from an unnamed girl. In the girl’s letter she wrote "do not let John get to you". Also found an old local map, with an X marked on it North of town, somewhere near the coast. It was identified as the Haunted Mansion location. The Haunted Mansion is North of town. Gnolls live over there and the bridge is out over the river. Rick had spent a lot of his time riding and was friendly with Deputy Andy. Got a recent picture of Rick. The Greens do not think he ran away. He had a mission to prove something to someone the last couple of days. Mr. Green really misses the horse that Rick took.

Went to Anna Brown’s parents dairy farm. Again met by a field hand who took us to the house. Met Mrs. Brown, a portly, jovial woman who loves to offer milkshakes. She said Anna had been gone for 5 days and thinks she ran off with Rick. When Anna left she took a flat bed horse carriage, but they didn’t notice anything else missing. In her room, found letters from Rick who also mentioned John. Anna had been upset that Rick was missing, on the last day she was here, she came home from school and was determined about something. The next morning she was gone. Anna’s best friend at school is Vesla White.

Went to Vesla’s home - her Dad is the carpenter. Vesla didn’t want us to go into the house, but would talk on the porch. Anna hadn’t said anything to her, but Vesla had seen Anna yelling at John Malet before she left. She didn’t know why Anna was yelling at John or anything about the Haunted House.

Went to talk to John Malet again, Father was present. He admitted liking Anna, but did not like Rick. Brought in little brother Jack, who confirmed that Anna and John were arguing. John admitted daring Rick to get a Gargoyle head from the Haunted Mansion. Aspen excuses himself from the room, and Aune goes and helps make tea with Mrs. Malet. Aspen searches John’s room and finds 50 crown and a long elf sword. Its getting late so we go back to town.

Meet with the fishermen and tell them that we had found acid burns on the dead fish that

were given to us to examine. One of the fisherman says that he has seen an oily slick on the water. Recommended that they start fishing in deeper water until we find the cause of the acid burns and also pointed out fishing was better South of town. Asked them to keep an eye out for anything that might help explain the acid burns. They reported that the Kelp seems OK, and River Fishing was also OK. Bought the fishermen a round of drinks. Some young fisherman offer to go North along the coast and look for anything suspicions. They will fly a red flag, and gave us a whistle to signal them if we see them from shore.

Before we headed to the hotel, took a look at the Turell who was still locked in one of the sheriff’s cell. He was doing much better and the doctor had been to see him.

Day 2:

Next morning is the Turell’s hearing with the Magistrate. The office smells awful from the sea dragon’s decomposing body, which is promptly removed to another building. Magistrate O’Connor hears the evidence, but will not release the Elf because of John Malet’s earlier testimony. However, he will release Turell into our custody as long as we are investigating the disappearances. We decide to take Turell with us to the Haunted Mansion.

Headed North along the rode out of town with Turell. When we got near the Wild Women’s yard, we could see a flat bed horse carriage in the yard and so investigate to see if it is Anna’s. We see long dead Gnoll heads mounted on the barn and several ferocious dogs heading towards us. Aspen tames the ferocious dogs in the yard and the Wild Women confirms it is Anna’s cart - she had asked her to watch it and her horse then headed towards the river. Back on the road, we can see the cart tracks going North to the river and then returning to the Wild Woman’s yard. Turell finds a foot pump for a raft at the river’s edge. Decide to spend the night on this side of the river as Wild Woman report the Gnolls are active. Head to the friendly Parishioner’s farm just South of Wild Woman’s. Parishioners receive us and feed us well. Calelith provides a lesson on tolerance. Confirm Anna used to visit the Wild Woman and was her friend.

Day 3:

Next morning cross the river uneventfully. On the other side we find the raft and can see old human skulls hanging from the tree. We bury the skulls and perform a ceremony. We head North along the road. Although Calelith is checking for traps, his horse steps on a Caltrop and throws Calelith. He nimbly avoids any damage and Aspen treats the Horse. We find 6 old rusty Caltrops and pick them up. Still checking for traps, we continue North. Calelith stumbles across a buried trap, but jumps out of the way. In the hole are new spikes, blood, and part of a bridle. We decide to leave the horses at the Parishioners. So we return to the River, Aspen crosses with the horses and returns on the raft.

When we get back to the trap, Calelith sees 1 Gnoll guarding the path and 5 restoring the trap. When they head into the woods, we follow. Calelith inadvertently sets off an alarm and tries to make it look like a branch had fallen on it instead while quickly retreating. We get off this trail by about 100’ and stand defensively. There are 4 Gnolls on the trail and 2 coming towards us off the trail. Calelith uses his "make audible noise" spell to make it sound like we are running towards the road. The Gnolls fall for it and run by us without noticing. We continue along the trail, but we trip another alarm. There are now 3 Gnolls coming at us from their camp. We can see several young Gnolls and one really big one in their camp. There is no sign of either Rick or Anna ever being there. We start to leave going parallel to the road and perpendicular to the trail and stumble across a small Gnoll. Calelith’s sleep spell works and we continue on our way hearing the Gnolls behind us. After diagonalling towards the road, we run across a Mansion road and take it east to the main road. Farther up along the main road, we see a clearing and in the clearing is a horse - Rick’s horse. The saddle is askew and the horse is beat up. Aspen works his animal charm and so we take it with us to the Haunted Mansion road. It is getting dark and the Gnolls are still following us in the distance.

Conclusion:
