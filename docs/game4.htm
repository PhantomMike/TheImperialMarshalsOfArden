Game #4 Summary

Play Date: January 14, 2000

Players in Attendance:
Mike, Diane, Kevin, Don, & Bryon

Dinner:
Pasta - Mike (Thanks Mike)

Game Time Line:
Start: May 23, 1919 (Near intersection of abandoned Main North Road and Haunted Mansion Road)
Stop: May 24, 1919 about 9:30 AM (In secret cellar of Haunted House)
Elapsed time: 1 day

Non Player Character:
Don: Turell - Elf (High Elf), Fighter (Non-Citizen Civilian)

Encounters:
Gnolls, six (killed in battle)
Human Thugs, five (Four killed in battle, one unconscious in custody)

Game Highlights:

Day 1:

Pursued by Gnolls

The party was still being pursued by the gnolls. Rather than confront the gnolls, the party decided to continue on to the haunted mansion. The party still had the Green’s prize horse. Aspen and Calelith lead the party attempting to detect traps along the way.

Calelith discovered a trip wire attached to a poison needle trap. He removed the poison needed and gave it to Aune to investigate. She determined the needle was tipped with a non-lethal poison. She kept the needle.

While traveling along the road, an apparition appeared in the road in front of the party and warned "turn back or you will meet your death". It then decomposes and disappeared before their very eyes.

Aspen detected the tracks of a small number of humans and gnolls that had traveled the road together within the last few days.

The party encountered a mysterious moss patch on the road and decided to go around it.

The Haunted Mansion

Towards the end of the road the party finally saw the mansion on top of bluff overlooking the ocean. The road lead to a main gate in an eight foot wall that surrounded the mansion.

Calelith poked his head through hole in the wall and noticed four skeletons standing motionless behind the wall by the gate. Aune noted that the skeletons did not radiate undead. The party avoided the skeletons by climbing through a breach in the wall.

Aspen determined that the gnoll and human tracks he had seen earlier lead to a side door of the mansion but the sun had set and it became too dark for him to track any further.

Aune and Calelith investigated the mansion by looking in its windows while Aspen and Turell kept watch out for the gnolls Aune and Calelith did not see any signs of light or life in the mansion but Aune noticed the floor on the north side of house was warmer than the rest of the house. Aune also sensed some minor undead under the south side of the house

Gnoll Attack

Six gnolls came up the road. The party took up a defensive positions behind the wall.

When the gnolls were in range of his spells, Calelith stood on the wall, announced in the gnoll language that they were Imperial Marshals and ordered the gnolls to surrender. However, the gnolls did not oblige and four of them charge the party while the remaining two began to shoot arrows and throw spears. Calelith cast color spray and the four charging gnolls were all knocked unconscious. The rest of the party shot it out with the remaining two gnolls. Protected by his shield spell, Calelith stood on the wall and taunted the remaining gnolls in an attempt to draw their fire. It worked and the two remaining gnolls were quickly gunned down by the party. The party tied up the four remaining gnolls who soon regained consciousness. Though Calelith tried to reason with the them, the gnolls seemed bent on destroying the party. Because of their size, it was obvious that the ropes would not hold the gnolls for long. After an extensive ethical debate, the party decided their only reasonable alternative was to "dispatch" the remaining gnolls else they would certainly attempt to kill the party or other innocents in the future. Aspen killed the gnolls and the party dropped their bodies off the cliff.

The party decided it was too late to investigate the mansion that night and decided to make camp just outside the mansion walls. They took turns taking watch in case more gnolls showed up but the night past uneventful.

Day 2:

Investigating the Haunted Mansion

The party rose early to investigate the mansion. They entered the side door noted earlier.

Upon entering the mansion, a voice from above bellowed out "Welcome to your doom!" Aune still did not detect any evil or undead on this side of the house. Calelith noticed the that the voice appeared to be coming from a wizard’s "magic mouth" spell and surmised that the "haunted" mansion was a ruse and that some sort of wizard had cast a bunch of spells in the area to make the mansion appear haunted.

The party moved through the house modestly investigating each room they past. While in the kitchen a poisonous centipede leaped from the sink onto Calelith’s back. Fortunately Aune and Aspen were able to knocked it off and kill it before it could do any harm. Other centipedes could be heard from the sink so the moved on to another room. The party encountered their first locked door to a room off the kitchen. Calelith successfully picked the lock and the party entered the pantry and quickly noticed that this room had been swept. A stair case lead down to the cellar and the party proceeded down the stairs. Another voice from above bellowed out "welcome to your doom" . The party ignored the voice and proceeded.

The room smelled of something rotting. It was dark so Aune took out her flashlight and Aspen his lantern. The room was a wine cellar with empty wine racks along the wall and a couple of bins by the west wall. A body of a man lay in the middle of the room. The man was wearing plate mail and had a sword. But his belt seemed to have been removed. The party investigated the body and determined that he probably had been dead for a couple of weeks and that his plate mails was still in very good shape.

Aspen and Calelith began to search for secret doors. Calelith found one on the west wall and Aspen figured out how to open it.

Battle with the Cellar Thugs

Upon opening the secret door, the party noticed light and voices coming from the other side. The party quietly opened the door and Calelith snuck in to investigate. Calelith saw five armed humans sitting at a near by table playing a board game. The room was large with 10 bunks lining the west wall. The party decided on an attack plan and went into action.

Calelith ordered step into the room and announced that the party were Imperial Marshals and ordered the humans to surrender. The humans were startled, stood up and began to draw weapons. Calelith cast another color spray but unfortunately this time he only knocked out one the opponents. Aune immediately followed using a "command" spell and ordered another of the humans to fall asleep. Aspen and Turell closed for hand to hand combat with their long swords. Of the remaining three humans, one pulled a sword and the other two pulled out pistols.

Aspen attacked the sword wielding human and delivered a near fatal blow and knocked him off his feet. At the same time Calelith cast a shrink spell on the sword wielding human and shrunk him to nearly half his size. Turell Landed a solid blow on one of the humans with a pistol while Aune shot and wounded the other with her revolver. The two humans returned fire seriously injuring both Aspen and Turell. The battle continued between the party and the two remaining. Aune and Calelith fired their revolvers while Aspen and Turell fought hand to hand with their swords. Then when of the humans critically wounded Aspen. A bullet passed through his chest and out his back. Aspen fell to the floor unconscious. Then Turell was again seriously injured to the point he also collapsed to the floor. But fortunately Turell remained conscious. But at that same point, the party had managed to kill one of the gunmen. Enraged by gunning down of his companion. Calelith pulled his out his rifle but before he could use it, he was shot and seriously injured by the remaining gunman. Knowing that the next shoot would most likely kill him, he dove for cover. The remaining gunman took his next shot at Aune he hit her but his bullet bounced off her armor. Aune notice the injured and shrunken human that had previously had a sword was pulling out a shotgun and she shot and killed him. The human that Aune had commanded to sleep was waking up and Turell, still on the floor wounded, reached over and took the humans revolver, pointed it at a grenade on the humans belt and told him not to move.

Aune gunned down the remaining gunman. The previously sleeping human decided to attempted to pick up one of the revolver on the ground. Turell shot at him but missed, fortunately Aune also shooting at him and hit him with a critical shot.

At the conclusion of the battle, four of the humans were dead and one was still unconscious. But Aspen was unconscious with a critical chest wound and both Calelith and Turell are seriously wounded. Aune used here two remaining spells to heal Aspen. This stabilized his condition but he did not regain consciousness. Calelith tied up the remaining unconscious human. Aune laid-on-hands healing on Turell and used her medic abilities and both Turell and Calelith.

The party notice that their are two other doors leading out of the room. The one that leads in the direction of the undead that Aune senses is marked with a danger sign. The other door is unremarkable. There is also a stair case at the other end of the room leading up to a trap door in the ceiling.

At Game Conclusion

The party is in a serious situation. Though not immediately threatened, each is at risk. At the moment Aspen is unconscious with a serious chest would that will limit his abilities until he recovers. Calelith is still seriously wounded with little spell ability remaining for the day. Turell is also seriously wounded and would not last long in another fire fight. Though uninjured, Aune has used up all her healing and spells for the day. All this and it’s not even 10 o’clock in the morning.
