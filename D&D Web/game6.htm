Game #6 Summary

Play Date: ?, 2000

Players in Attendance:

Game Time Line:

        Stop: May 25, 1919 AM (just before sunrise, at beach below Haunted House)

Elapsed time: a few hours

Characters in party:

Player Characters:
        Aspen, Aune, Calelith, & Halse

Non Player Characters:
        Turell - Elf (High Elf), Fighter
        Anna - Human

Encounters:
        Sabeth - Smuggler leader, magic user
        Larry - Human thug prisoner
        Ned Flanders- Smuggler (Syndicate bookkeeper)
        Lizard Men - (four in the sea cave and one guarding the ship)
        Crew of Sea Shadow

Game Highlights:

At start of game

Aspen and Calelith have just defeated a couple of gnolls in the sea cave under the haunted mansion.  The rest of the party has taken up a defensive position in the mansion cellar.

 The Sea Cave

The Party reunites in the sea cave. The party finds a locked desk and sees some lizard men hiding in a freshwater pool in the cave.  The party also finds a pile of contraband smuggled from the League of Realms.

 Battle with Sabeth

Sabeth and Ned return to cave with skiff full of cargo.    Sabeth is invisible and two lizard men are pulling the skiff.  A fight ensues, the lizard me retreat, Ned hides and Sabeth attacks.  Once visible, Calelith color sprays Sabeth knocking him out.  Sabeth is tied up.  The cargo on the skiff are boxes of rifles, pistols, and grenades.  Sabeth has a coded log book on him. Calelith deciphers it and determines that there is more cargo on the ship to be brought ashore. 

Disaster at Sea

The party decides to go out to the ship and try and sink it by attaching a box of grenades to its hull.  Aune takes Ned on the skiff to impersonate smugglers coming to pick up the next load.  Calelith, & Halse row out in another boat and try and sneak up on the ship and attach the box of grenades to the hull.  Aspen & Turell stay on shore to guard Anna and the prisoners.  Aune and Ned get to the ship first.  Aune tries to convince the crew she there to pick up the cargo.  They are suspicious but begin to unload the cargo.  During the exchange it becomes clear that the crew does not like Sabeth.

While Calelith & Halse are trying to connect the box of grenades to the hull of the ship, a lizardman guarding the ship in the water tips over Calelith’s & Halse’s boat sending the box of grenades to the bottom.  Calelith hides under the capsized boat as it floats away.  Halse tries to board the ship.  Hearing the commotion on the other side of the ship, Aune goes on the offensive and throws a grenade on to the ship.  However, the grenade bounces off the side of the ship and falls back into the skiff with Aune and Ned.  Both dive overboard as the skiff blows up.  Aune and Ned then swim back to shore. 

Halse is caught while trying to board the ship and surrenders to the crew.  Halse explains to the crew that he is just looking for Rick Green.  The crew decides he is not a threat and return his weapons.  The crew says Rick is not with them but they can take Halse to him.  Halse begins to negotiate his passage to Rick. 

At game conclusion

Aune, Calelith, and Ned are swimming back to the sea cave. 

Halse is on board the ship negotiating passage to Rick Green. 

Aspen and Turell are in the sea cave guarding Anna and the prisoners.

Experience Points Gained: 500
