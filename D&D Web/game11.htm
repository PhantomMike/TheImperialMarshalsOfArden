Game #11 Summary

Play Date:  October 13, 2000
 
Players in Attendance: 
    Mike, Diane, Don, & Bryon
 
Dinner:
    Way cool Lasagna and Ice Cream - Don
 
Game Time Line:
    Start: June 8, 1919  late afternoon (At Rodenberg Cathedral)
    Stop:  June 12, 1919  just after sunset (Landing at Arden Airport)
    Elapsed time:  four days
 
The Party:
    Imperial Marshals from the State of Arden - Special Investigations Unit
    Mike:  Aspen Eliyzer
    Diane:  Aune Vinje
    Kevin:  Calelith Tanduil
    Don:  Halse Keenith Greenman
 
Encounters:
    Rodenberg Cathedral:
        Rodenberg refugees and three hiding Zair soldiers
 
    Rodenberg:
        Colonel Brennen
        Major Raquel Scott
        Captain Cane - Imperial Wizard Warrior
        Several members of Black Force
 
    Eddington:
        Agent Zachery of the Secret Service
        Professor Biddilo - Hobbit Anthropologist at Eddington’s Arden Academy
        Professor Biddilo’s cousin, Hummel, an Archeologist (by phone)
 
    Roahock Imperial Airfield:
        Maloe - Grey Elf Cleric, and his two chaotic High Elf henchmen
        Several Imperial Soldiers and airfield personnel
 
Game Highlights:
 
At game start
 Calelith, Aune, and Halse are completing their investigation of the alter room on the other side of the teleportation gateway under the Gnome alter in the Rodenberg Cathedral.   Aspen is waiting on the cathedral side of the gate.  Aune, Calelith, and Aspen are all badly injured.
 
Escaping Rodenberg
As Calelith, Aune, and Halse finish their investigations of the alter room on the other side of the teleportation gateway, Aspen begins to hear and feel  the vibrations of distant explosions.  Aspen yells up to the Rodenberg garrison soldier standing guard and asks what’s going on.  The soldier tells him “now it’s our turn” implying that Realm forces are beginning to retake the city.   After the rest of the party returns through the gateway, Aspen tells them “it’s time to leave”.  The party quickly returns to Major Scott who is still waiting outside in the Cathedral guard shack.  Realm forces can be seen parachuting into the city.  The party and Major Scott avoid Realm sniper fire as the return to make their report to Colonel Brennen.
 
The party inform Colonel Brennen of what they’ve learned.  The party tells the Colonel that as long as ancient gnome key remains out of enemy hands, then the artifact should be safe.  The Colonel is uncomfortable leaving the artifact in a Realm city.  Now that the party believes they know how to remove the artifact, the Colonel suggest to the party that they consider returning to Rodenberg to retrieve it and keep it from enemy hands.  Captain Cane returns the ancient gnome key to the party.  The Colonel believes the cleric will be able to track the key and the party agrees to be paranoid.  The Colonel tells the party that the safest place for either the key or the artifact is the Imperial Palace in Arden. 
 
At the conclusion of the discussion, the colonel orders the party to their sea plane at the river port.  A squadron of Imperial fighters will be there shortly to provide air cover for the parties escape.  He sends Major Scott with.  The party evades sniper fire and reach the river port.  There are two seaplanes with engines already warmed up.  One plane is for the party and the other is to evacuate casualties.  One Black Force member with a flame thrower and two other injured members stand guard.  Halse goes to the roof of a burned out warehouse to keep watch until they can take off. 
 
Halse sees and approaching Realm tank.  He gestures to the party and guards.  Halse and the Black Force soldier with the flame thrower move into the buildings and to try and ambush the tank.  As the tank approaches, Halse notice the Black Force soldier on the other side of the road.  Halse leaps out and fires several shots at the tank.  Though this does no damage he does distract the tank and make it turn towards him.  The Black Force soldiers shoots flames at the back of the tank causing it to catch fire.  The tank stops engulfed in flames.  As one of the crew begin to exit, Halse takes a shot at him, forcing the crewmen back into the burning tank.  However a Realm sniper spots Halse and shoots at him but misses.
 
The party hears the approach of the squadron of Imperial fighters.  Halse returns to the plane and they take off leaving Rodenberg, Colonel Brennen, Captain Cane, Black Force, and Cathedral refugees to their own destinies.  The flight to Eddington is uneventful.
 
Back in Eddington
The party takes shelter at Eddington’s Arden Academy where they healed by Arden clerics.  Agent Zachery of the Imperial Secret Service checks in on the party to see how they are doing.  Aune asks him what he knows about gnomes.  Zachery says he doesn’t know much but there is a Hobbit anthropologist at the academy who may know something.  Aune asks a few other academy researchers about gnomes and again they suggest she see the Hobbit, Professor Biddilo.  So the party goes to visit Professor Biddilo. 
 
Professor Biddilo
As soon as the party enter Professor Biddilo’s jumbled office, the Professor immediately recognizes Aune’s Grey Elf features and begins to question her about her heritage.  During this exchange, Halse finds a small statuette of what appears to be one of the “Ancient Ones” described on the walls under the Gnome temple in Rodenberg.  The professor tells the party that the statuette is a archeological item that was a gift from his cousin who is on an archeological dig in the southern desert region. 
 
Professor Biddilo tells the party that most of the gnomes have died out over the centuries.  Those that remain prefer to remain isolated.  When this region became populated by humans, the Gnome settlements moved to more isolated areas.  He knows that some of the gnomes went to Dartmen.  The professor tells the party about Dartmen.
 
The Story of Dartmen
Dartmen was small country on a peninsula southwest of the Arden Empire.  60 years ago the empire began the forced assimilation of Dartmen into Arden.  As imperial forces closed in on Castle Dartmen, in a desperate attempt to save her kingdom, the Cleric Queen Holara turned from the Holy Church and embraced the evils of the underworld.  She summoned various demons and other unholy creature to do battle with the empire.  However, assisted by Crusaders from the Holy Church, Imperial forces defeated and destroyed Queen Holara’s monsters.  Castle Dartmen fell, but Queen Holara escaped. 
 
Though Dartmen was now under Imperial control, the ramifications of Queen Halara’s dealings with demons swept over what remained of her kingdom.  Sickness, droughts, and fires plagued the lands.  In desperation, most of the human population emigrated to other parts of the empire leaving only a handful of hamlets and small garrison of imperial soldiers stationed in keeps throughout Dartmen.      
 
With the human population gone, Dartmen became a haven for those within the Empire who would not be assimilated into its culture.  Large bands of goblins as well a small number of gnomes settled there. 
 
The Key
Aune pulls out the ancient gnome key.  The professor immediately recognizes it and pulls out a Zair Royal Museum reference book.  There he shows the party a photo of the Gnome key.  The book says the key was found in a ancient gnome alter in Zaire, several hundred miles from Rodenberg.  The book says the key is supposed to be on display at the Zair Royal Museum.
 
Professor Biddilo cousin
Aune asks the Professor what he know about the Ancient Ones.  He says that this the expertise of his cousin, Hummel.  Hummel is an archeologist doing digs in the desert region in the South.  The Professor says they can call him by phone. 
Once on the phone, Hummel is eager to talk to the party.  Among other things, he tells the party that the Ancient Ones used teleportation gates and after their demise, some of the elfin and gnome tribes captured the gates and used them for their own purposes.  In addition, though he knows they existed, he as not found a single Ancient One tomb.  Hummel invites the party to his digs.  “We could always use the protection of Marshals, the religious unrest in the area makes digging here dangerous.” 
 
The sea elf temple and the books
Aune asks the Professor what he know about the Zair archeology investigation of the Sea Elf temple on Island Keep.  He knows nothing about it but is very interested because the Sea Elves are another area of his study.  He is trying to determine why the Sea Elf population has died out.  After Aune tells him what she knows, he decides to sneak over the border and join the dig.  Aune gives the Professor the books she borrowed from the Zair archeologist on Island Keep so he can return them.  The professor in turn gives Aune the Zaire Royal Museum book.
 
Time to Leave
Later in the day, Aune gets a funny feeling that she can not explain.  Aspen says with the Grey Elf Cleric after them,  it’s probably not a good idea to stay in one place too long.  The party decides to catch a flight to Arden to take the key to the Imperial Palace.
 
The party goes to see Major Scott.  She says she is busy trying to figure a way to get Brennen and Black Force out of Rodenberg.  The military can no longer get air transport to the city so Colonel Brennen, Captain Cane, and the forty remaining members of Black Force will have to find another way out.  However she quickly arranges passage on a large military transport flying out in the morning for Arden.
 
The party catches the transport and the first leg of the trip is uneventful.   However, the transport has to make a fuel and maintenance stop at Roahock Imperial Airfield.  Aune stays with the plane but the rest of the party goes to eat at the airfield cafeteria.
 
He’s back
Aune senses the approach of the cleric and sees a cloud like substance descending on the airfield. Aune surmises that this is the cleric traveling in cloud form.  She yells for the rest of the party.  Aspen orders a group of soldiers to prepare to defend the party from attack.  Aune casts a dust devil to contain the cloud, but the cleric and two high elves materialize from the cloud.  Aspen orders the soldiers to shoot.  At first they are confused and only a couple actually shoot but do little damage.   Calelith color sprays the three elves and manages to stuns one of the high elves.  Aspen commandeers a plane and tries to get it started.  Halse shoots his shot gun at the cleric and does a moderate amount of damage to him.  Aune retreats to the plane.  One of the high elves charges Aune.  Two soldiers try and grab him as he goes by but the elf easily evades their grasps.  The other soldiers fire at the stunned high elf and the cleric.  They do serious damage to the stunned elf but little to the cleric.  The cleric casts a hold spell at Aune but she makes her saving throw.  Calelith uses hand wand to cast a giant hand to attack the cleric then retreats to the airplane.  Aspens has a difficult time getting the plane to power up.  Aune, standing on the plane, engages in a sword fight with the high elf and takes several hits.  Halse shoots the high elf from behind doing a little damage but failing to distract him from his combat with Aune. The cleric cast a command spell at Aune to “Jump”.  She hears it and jumps up and falls off the plane.  However, she quickly turns and jumps back on to the plane, taking another sword hit from the attacking high elf.  The soldiers continue to fire at the cleric and the stunned elf.  Again the stunned elf is hit and the cleric is not.  The badly wounded high elf recovers from being stunned and promptly disappears.  The cleric began to cast another spell but the giant hand hits him knocking him down and interrupting the spell.
 
With all of the party aboard the mail plane, Aspen finally gets its engine powered up and begins to take off.  The attacking high elf makes a desperate final attack on the tail of the plane to try and stop the party from taking off but misses.  Aune takes a final shot at the attacking high elf with her shot gun and inflicts the last hit of the battle. The party escapes leaving the soldiers to deal with the cleric and the remaining high elf.
 
At Game Conclusion
Aspen is piloting the commandeered mail plane with the rest of the party as passengers.   They have just landed at an airfield outside the Capital City of Arden.
